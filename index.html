Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes. If you don't find your answer, feel free to contact us.

What is the typical project timeline?
Project timelines can vary depending on the complexity and scope of the work. A typical one-page site like this might take X to Y weeks from start to finish, including design, development, and revisions. We'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.
How do you handle revisions?
We believe in a collaborative process. We typically include a set number of revision rounds at key stages of the project (e.g., after initial design, after development). We'll discuss this with you to ensure you're happy with the final product.
Do you provide ongoing support for CCTV systems?
Yes, we offer various ongoing support and maintenance packages for CCTV installations to ensure your system remains up-to-date, secure, and performs optimally. We can discuss these options based on your needs.
What information do you need for a CCTV quote?
To provide an accurate quote for a CCTV system, we typically need to understand the layout of the premises, key areas to monitor, desired camera types (e.g., indoor/outdoor, night vision), recording duration, and any specific security concerns you have. A site survey may be beneficial.
