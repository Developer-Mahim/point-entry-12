Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes. If you don't find your answer, feel free to contact us.

What services do you offer?
We specialize in:
24/7 Professional Monitoring
Smart Building Automation (lighting, HVAC, security integration)
Access Control Systems (keycards, biometrics, mobile credentials)

How does 24/7 monitoring work?
Our team monitors your property in real-time via connected cameras/sensors, with instant alerts sent to you and emergency responders if needed.
Can I integrate new cameras with my existing system?
Yes! We design solutions to upgrade and seamlessly integrate with your current setup-no rip-and-replace required.
What's the difference between analog and IP cameras?
Analog: Cost-effective, uses coaxial cables (lower resolution).
IP: Digital, higher resolution, remote access, and Al features (e.g., facial recognition).
Do you offer remote access to surveillance feeds?
Yes. View live or recorded footage anytime via our secure mobile app or digital panel.
How long is video footage stored?
Customizable plans (typically 7-30 days), with options for cloud or on-site storage.
Are your systems hack-proof?
We use military-grade encryption, regular firmware updates, and VLAN-segregated networks to minimize vulnerabilities.
What's smart building automation?
Centralized control of security, lighting, temperature, and more-all programmable and accessible remotely.
Do you service commercial and residential properties?
Yes. We tailor solutions for homes, businesses, industrial sites, and government facilities.
How do I get started?
